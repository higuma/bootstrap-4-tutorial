Nav

A navbar usually has a nav and also behaves responsively with a toggler. A nav is set inside of a collapsing wrapper. A toggler collapses a nav when the viewport width is smaller than the specified breakpoint.

Responsive behaviors

A navbar acts responsively with a toggler.

  • Switchs layouts (expanded/collapsed) with a specific grid breakpoint (lt, md, lg, or xl).
  • On expanded, shows all items (brand, nav, text, form, etc.) horizontally in the navbar
  • On collapsed, hides items and shows a triple bar icon
    • By clicking an icon, shows(expands) collapted items (stacked vertically)
